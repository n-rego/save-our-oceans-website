THE PROBLEM

Each year, billions of pounds of trash and other pollutants enter the ocean. Approximately every square mile of ocean has more than 46,000 pieces of plastic floating in it.


Over 100,000 marine animals die every year from plastic entanglement and ingestion. It’s estimated that by the year 2050, there will be more plastic in the ocean than fish (by weight). Even though we are currently at the worst state ever, we still have a chance to fight it.

OUR MISSION

With the solo crossing, we plan on fighting for ocean conservation. this means we will have an active role, teaching and actually cleaning the ocean. together with the physical impact of our cleanup, we will also work to educate children about ocean conservation and why it’s fundamental to take an active role. during our campaign, we will have physical and digital brand activations where we will be able to reach a big audience.
